Facebook

This study uses your Facebook data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Login to Facebook.

If you have not already, please log into your Facebook account.

On the main screen, select the options menu in the upper-right area of your screen. From the menu, select Settings.

2. Navigate to data request page.

On the General Account Settings page, select Your Facebook Information from the list on the left side of the page.

On the next page, select the Download Your Information option.

From the next page, you may export your data from Facebook.

3. Download your information.

The Download Your Information page allows you to select the parameters for your data download.

For this study, please specify the following options:

  • Date Range: Select a time duration from 1 year ago to the present.
  • Format: Select JSON.
  • Media Quality: Select Medium.

In the Your Information, toggle the Select All option in the upper-right so that all of the items are deselected. After the items have been cleared, for this study, select the following content types:

With all of the items selected, press the Create File button.

Facebook will display a message indicating that it is creating your file.

4. Upload your information.

After Facebook compiles your data file, it will send you an e-mail with a link and notify you within Facebook that your data is ready to be downloaded. Follow the provided instructions to retrieve your file.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
