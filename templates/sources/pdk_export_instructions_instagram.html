Instagram

This study uses your Instagram data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Navigate to data request page.

Visit the data request page to begin the data download process. (You may be prompted to login if needed.)

Confirm your e-mail and password on to continue.

Instagram will confirm that your data download is processing.

2. Download your data.

Once your data is finished processing, Instagram will send you an e-mail with a download link.

Tap the Download Data button in the e-mail to go to the download page.

On the web page, tap the Download Data link to download a ZIP file with your full Instagram history to your local disk.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
