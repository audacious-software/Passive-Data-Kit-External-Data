Tumblr

This study uses your Tumblr data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Request data.

Navigate to your Tumblr privacy page.

At the bottom of the page, select the Request Privacy Data button. Tumblr will begin compiling your data and inform you that you will receive an e-mail when the data is ready.

2. Read e-mail notification.

After Tumblr has finished compiling your data archive, you will receive an e-mail with a link to your data.

3. Download your data.

Return to your Tumblr privacy page.

At the bottom of the page, select the Download button. Tumblr will download your data to your local machine.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
