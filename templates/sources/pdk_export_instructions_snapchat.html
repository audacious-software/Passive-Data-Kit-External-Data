Snapchat

This study uses your Snapchat data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Navigate to data request page.

Visit your Snapchat data page to begin the data download process. (You may be prompted to login if needed.)

At the bottom of the page, select the SUBMIT REQUEST button. Snapchat will begin compiling your data and inform you that you will receive an e-mail when the data is ready.

2. Read e-mail notification.

After Twitter has finished compiling your data archive, you will receive an e-mail with a link to your data.

Select the click here link to continue.

3. Download your data.

In the browser, download your data by selecting the link to the right of the Your Data is Ready column.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
