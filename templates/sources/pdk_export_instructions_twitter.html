Twitter

This study uses your Twitter data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Navigate to data request page.

Visit your settings page to begin the data download process. (You may be prompted to login if needed.)

In the right-most column, select Your Twitter data. Provide your password if prompted.

2. Request your data.

In the bottom of the right-most column, should see buttons allowing you to Request archive for your data. Select that button.

(If you have recently requested your Twitter data, this button may be Download archive. If this is the case, select that button and skip to step 4.)

When you request your data, you will see an on-screen notification informing you that Twitter is building your archive.

3. Read e-mail notification.

After Twitter has finished compiling your data archive, you will receive an e-mail with a link to your data.

Select the Download link to continue.

4. Download your data.

When your data is ready, you may download it.

Select the Download Data button to receive a copy of your Twitter data.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
