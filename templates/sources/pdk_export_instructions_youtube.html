YouTube

This study uses your YouTube data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Navigate to Google Takeout page.

Visit your Google Takeout page to begin the data download process. (You may be prompted to login if needed.)

Scroll down to the CREATE A NEW ARCHIVE section. Select the Deselect all link to uncheck all the available data types.

With all items deselected, scroll to the bottom of the page and select YouTube.

Select the Multiple formats button beneath the option. In the pop-up window, be sure that JSON is selected as the format for History. Close the pop-up by selecting the OK button at the bottom of the window.

Select the All YouTube data included button beneath the option. In the pop-up window, be sure that videos is not selected. This will create a much smaller file to download that Google will be able to create quickly.

Select the Next step button.

Keep the default settings and select the Create archive button. Google will begin compiling your data file.

2. Read e-mail notification.

After Google has finished compiling your data archive, you will receive an e-mail with a link to your data.

Select the Download archive link to continue.

3. Download your data.

From the Manage archives page, download the newly-created file.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
