Instructions

1. Request data.

Navigate to your LinkedIn settings. Scroll down to the Getting a copy of your data section.

Verify that the Download larger data archive option is selected and tap the Request archive button.

LinkedIn will begin compiling your data and send you an e-mail when that process is complete.

2. Read e-mail notification.

After LinkedIn has finished compiling your data archive, you will receive an e-mail with a link to your data.

3. Download your data.

You can return to your LinkedIn settings by tapping the link in the e-mail.

The form that you used to request your data will be faded out and the Request archive button will have changed to Download archive. Tap the button to download a your data.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
