WhatsApp

This study uses your WhatsApp data to do X, Y, and Z.

Please follow the instructions on the right to request your data.

Instructions

1. Open WhatsApp on your device and navigate to the settings.

Navigate to the settings and select the Account, then Request account info.

2. Request your data from the app.

From the Request account info page, tap the option to Request report. The option will change to reflect that the report request has been sent to the WhatsApp service.

3. Download WhatsApp data.

After WhatsApp is finished compiling your usage data, you will receive a notification on your device informing you that the report is ready.

Tap the notification to open WhatsApp.

The option on the Request account info screen has changed to Download report - tap that to begin. The Download Report option will change to Export report after the data has been downloaded to your device. Tap it again to share your data to your device.

After WhatsApp warns you to be careful with your data, select an option where WhatsApp can share the data. In the example below, we e-mail it to ourselves.

Once you have downloaded your file, follow the instructions in the e-mail you receive from us for a link and instructions for to upload your data to the study.
